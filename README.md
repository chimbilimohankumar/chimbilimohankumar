## About Me

Sr. DevOps Engineer with 8+ years of experience designing, automating, and scaling cloud-native infrastructure. I live at the intersection of reliability, automation, and observability, turning complex deployment challenges into smooth, repeatable pipelines.

I also love sharing what I learn with the community through content creation on YouTube, Medium, and Instagram.

- ☁️ I build and manage production-grade Kubernetes (AKS) clusters
- 🔁 I automate CI/CD with GitHub Actions and ArgoCD
- 📊 I obsess over monitoring with Grafana and Prometheus
- 🐍 I script my way out of everything with Python
